Rocky Mountain National Park — 3M Curve or Hidden Valley

romo_permits@nps.gov · (970) 586-1206 · $300 permit · APPLY IMMEDIATELY
Rocky Mountain National Park · Wildflower Season, August
  • 3M Curve: Dramatic open views of Longs Peak and the Continental Divide — wildflower meadows peak in August
  • Hidden Valley: Forested, intimate, completely private — max 30 people, serene alpine meadow setting
  • Only 60 Special Use Permits issued per month May–October — apply immediately via romo_permits@nps.gov
  • $300 non-refundable fee · back-up venue: Rocky Mountain Wedding Park (rockymountainweddingpark.com)
  • Colorado self-solemnization: no officiant or witnesses required — the permit + license is everything
  • Private car service from Riversong Inn recommended — Eddie Limo (eddielimo.com) · (303) 803-7363
5.0 ★★★★★  · RMNP visitor ratings + elopement photographer portfolios
Google Maps ↗
RMNP elopement photographers describe August ceremonies at 3M Curve as among the most visually dramatic in Colorado — Longs Peak rising directly behind the couple with wildflowers carpeting the meadow. Hidden Valley offers more intimate forest seclusion. Both sites are permit-only and feel genuinely private. The RMNP permit is the single most time-sensitive booking of any location in this guide — do not delay applying.
"The Rocky Mountains aren't the most traditional place to get married, but they are certainly the coolest. At 3M Curve, the photo opportunities are endless and the memories will last forever." — Alex Medvick Photography
⚠ URGENT — Most Time-Sensitive Booking in This Guide: Only 60 RMNP permits issued per month. Email romo_permits@nps.gov today. Attach your date (August 7, 2026), preferred site, and contact info. A backup venue reservation at Rocky Mountain Wedding Park is strongly recommended.

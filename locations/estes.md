    - Google Review Summary: Highly rated for amazing gluten-free options and fantastic fried chicken with creative cocktails. Located in The Stanley Hotel's original Carriage House. Reviewers consistently praise the welcoming atmosphere, attentive staff, standout fried chicken, buffalo chicken sandwiches, and green chili mac n cheese. Some mention occasional slow service but worth the wait.

# Palisade/Grand Junction Elopement Weekend

## Lodging
- [The Hotel Melrose](https://www.thehotelmelrose.com/), Historic 1908 boutique hotel in downtown Grand Junction, 16 stylish suites with kitchenettes, onsite cocktail bar, ~$150-200/night

## Ceremony Location
- [Colorado National Monument](https://www.nps.gov/colm/), Dramatic red rock canyons and overlooks 10 minutes from downtown, requires NPS special use permit, ~$150-300

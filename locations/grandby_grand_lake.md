## Saturday Adventure
- [Grand Adventure Balloon Tours](https://grandadventureballoon.com/) - Fraser Valley sunrise flight with Continental Divide views, champagne toast and breakfast included, Fodor's Best 10 in US, ~$400–600 for two
    - Photo: https://grandadventureballoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/no-watermark-3web.jpg
    - Google Review Summary: Captain Jack provides exceptional hot air balloon experiences with over 30 years of expertise and a perfect 30-year safety record. Passengers consistently praise his knowledge of the Fraser Valley, professional demeanor, and ability to put anxious flyers at ease; trips include breakfast and champagne. One reviewer reported poor communication regarding weather cancellations.
    - Google rating: 4.0 stars

## Thursday Dinner
- [Brickhouse 40](https://www.yelp.com/biz/brickhouse-40-granby) - Downtown Granby, elevated comfort food in historic brick building, part saloon/part restaurant, ~$80–120
    - Google Review Summary: Located in a historic 1927 brick building reopened in 2018, this modern American restaurant features friendly service and creative menu items like firecracker shrimp and the Brickhouse Bowl. The cozy atmosphere with mountain vibe and dog-friendly patio are assets, though some reviewers note occasional slow service and underwhelming burgers when busy.
    - Google rating: 4.2 stars
- [Maverick's Grille](https://www.destinationgranby.com/restaurants/) - Western-themed with locally sourced ingredients, famous green chili and wild game meatloaf, ~$80–120
    - Google Review Summary: Known for gourmet burgers and excellent green chili, Maverick's consistently receives praise for flavorful, well-prepared dishes and craft Colorado beers. Reviewers commend the warm ambiance and attentive service, though some report billing accuracy issues and suggest the green chili skews toward "gringo" style rather than traditional New Mexico green chili.
    - Google rating: 4.2 stars

## Saturday Brunch & Dinner
- Brunch in Grand Lake - Casual lakeside options after balloon ride, ~$40–60
- [One Love Rum Kitchen](https://gograndlake.com/restaurants-dining/) - Caribbean flavors in Grand Lake, fish tacos and rum drinks with relaxed vibe, ~$60–90
    - Google Review Summary: This Caribbean-inspired "beach bar in the mountains" is highly rated across platforms (4.5-4.7 stars) for creative dishes like pork belly tacos and Mahi Mahi tacos, unique cocktails, and exceptional bartender service. Happy hour (3-5 pm) offers 50% off many items, though some guests report lengthy waits for food even during slower periods.
    - Google rating: 4.5 stars
- [Sagebrush BBQ & Grill](https://gograndlake.com/restaurants-dining/) - Log cabin Old West decor, slow-smoked meats and house-made sides, ~$60–90
    - Google Review Summary: Voted "Grand Lake's #1 Restaurant," this BBQ spot is praised for generous portions, locally-sourced ingredients, and reasonable pricing, with pulled pork sandwiches and the Ridge Trail Sampler as standouts. Breakfast is particularly excellent, the dog-friendly outdoor seating is spacious, and service is consistently warm and attentive, though some note occasional food quality inconsistencies.
    - Google rating: 4.2 stars (ranked #3 of 22 restaurants on TripAdvisor)
